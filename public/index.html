Storyline Summary

After eating the Gomu Gomu no Mi, Monkey D. Luffy gains the ability to stretch like rubber. His dream is to find the legendary treasure "One Piece" left by the Pirate King Gol D. Roger and become the new Pirate King.

Together with his loyal crew — the Straw Hat Pirates — he sails the dangerous Grand Line, faces powerful Warlords, Emperors, and the World Government, forging unbreakable friendships along the way.
